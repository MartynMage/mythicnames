Elven Naming Tips

When creating your own elven names or choosing from this list, keep these principles in mind:

  • Emphasize vowels — Elven names flow with A, E, I, and soft consonants
  • Use common elven sounds — "Ael," "El," "Wen," "Iel," "Thar," "Lin"
  • Consider the subrace — Wood elves might have nature-themed names, high elves might sound more formal
  • Add a family name — Elven surnames often describe lineage or home: "Starweaver," "Moonbrook," "Silverglade"
