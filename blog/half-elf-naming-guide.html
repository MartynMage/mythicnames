Half-elves walk between two worlds, and their names often reflect this duality. Some embrace their elven heritage, others their human side, and many find unique names that honor both.

Tips for Blending Names

  • Elven first name + Human surname (or vice versa)
  • Shortened elven names that sound human-friendly
  • Human names with elven suffixes (-iel, -wen, -orn)
  • Names that could work in either culture
