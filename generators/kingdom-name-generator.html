About Kingdom Names

A kingdom's name establishes its identity, culture, and place in your world. Good kingdom names feel authentic to their fictional culture while remaining memorable and pronounceable.

Common Naming Patterns

  • -ia suffix: Valeria, Astoria (Latin feel)
  • -land suffix: Nordland, Frostland (Germanic)
  • -heim suffix: Stormheim, Ironheim (Norse)
  • Compound names: Stormhold, Sunspire, Blackmoor
